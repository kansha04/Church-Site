About Us

A very warm welcome to Christ Centered Church, Leuven. We are a Bible teaching Christian community, devoted to expressing the reality of Jesus Christ in our lives, through serving and worshiping our Lord, caring and praying for one another, and reaching out to the world through the power and love of the Holy Spirit. Our passion is to love, serve and worship God our Lord, and to love others the way He taught us.

If you are looking for a place where you could not only experience an authentic community, but also be a part of a Christian family and the body of Christ, and if you are looking for an opportunity to learn about the extraordinary God, to build a personal relationship with Him as well as many lasting friendships with others like you, then you are most welcome to come and join us.

Christ Centered Church, Leuven, teaches the Word of God through Bible messages/Sunday service, home based Bible Study cell groups and prayer meetings, provides Sunday School activities for children and runs a Youth Group, as well as various annual Christian events, all with a contemporary praise and worship music and in a warm, relaxed atmosphere.

May the Lord God bless you and we are hoping to see you soon.

– Pastor Seth Sakyi-Gyinae
